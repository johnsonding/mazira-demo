A Great Summer with Big Data at Mazira

by Mei Rao
2013/09/11

It was late in the spring of 2013, when I received word from Mazira, a software company located in Iowa City, IA and focused on providing customized and industry specific data solutions, that I would be invited to take part in their my summer internship program to lead the digital marketing and SEO project. With more than five years marketing and sales working experience, I knew from the beginning that this would be a great opportunity for my entire career path.

It was a quick decision. I entered the program a few days later. After one day brief orientation, I started working on the top competitors’ analysis. Amazingly, I found that Mazira was the only company that is dedicated to designing customized software that helps companies to make the most out of the data they have, regardless the format and location of the file. The unique timeline-based platform makes Mazira stands out in millions of competitors in the industry. By saying this, I did not mean that there was no competition on the market. Actually, the competition is really fierce as the software industry is distinguished by its capacity for constant innovation. Anith Mathai, the CEO of Mazira, and Ross Johnson, the CTO and mastermind of Mazira, learned early on that there is a huge potential market for customized data solutions and quickly found that was exactly what their customers were looking for. Our first marketing campaign started with BondPDF, which is our first software designed especially for the financial industry. FactEntry, a financial service company based in London, was our first customer that benefits a lot from our data solutions. Using this as a breakthrough point, we collected user feedback from FactEntry to support our marketing on selected digital channels.

Within a month, we increased our Facebook “likes” by 625% and website traffic by 900%. The whole team was excited during out monthly digital marketing campaign meeting. We all believe that more successful stories are on the way.

Without a surprise, a part-time job for the school year was offered on my last day of work. Mazira values everyone’s talent and extra effort they contribute the company. I am so happy that I could continue to be a valued member of the team as I continue to take ownership of my work.

My message to future interns would be to take any opportunity that you have to learn as much as you can. Never hesitate to share your ideas and ask questions. At Mazira, new ideas are always welcome!

Mazira is a data and document management company. We build software to store and organize large data collections so that it can become useful. Data by itself isn't very useful unless it is distilled into useful information!
